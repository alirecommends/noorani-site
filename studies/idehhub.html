Brand Identity

Branding for Crowdfunding: Ideh Hub

Ideh Hub is a boutique startup consultancy platform providing leading-edge advisory services and financial analysis to the Iranian startup ecosystem.

My goal was to create an original, stylish, and memorable brand identity that captures the essence of ideation and the stability of a financial consultancy. (*"Ideh" in Persian is a loan word meaning idea).

Strategic Direction

To understand what direction the brand should take, I consulted with the company founders and brainstormed the main attributes they needed to convey to their two primary audiences: creative startup founders and serious financial investors.

Core Brand Attributes

Trustworthy Committed Discipline Cool Honest

Core Functions

  • Financial analysis
  • Capture new opportunities
  • Establish sustainable startups

The Typography Story

1. The Spark of an Idea

The central concept combines "idea" and "hub" by compressing the repetitive 'H'. The visual journey begins with the dot on the "i".

Here, the sans-serif "ideh" features a vibrant, modern style reminiscent of the unlimited, fluid nature of ideation. The idea shines like a light bulb.

2. Anchoring with Discipline

The design reaches its turning point when crossing to the "HUB's" more official and disciplined atmosphere: stable, trustworthy, and result-driven.

HUB is the serious side of the project. The serif font conveys the official, legal, and financial aspects of the consultancy. It is clean and concise. Hub captures and anchors the idea.

Iconography

A Monogram Beyond Initials

The company required an independent monogram that would complement its logotype and brand message for use in smaller spaces. I focused on the initials of Ideh & Hub (i + H).

  • Human Connection: The bridge on the 'H' and the figure-like shape of 'i' form a symbol of two figures interacting—representing shaking hands, exchanging ideas, or attracting investment.
  • Illuminated Minds: The heads of the two figures are lit up in sky blue, mirroring the creative spark established in the main logotype.
