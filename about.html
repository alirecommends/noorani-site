UX/UI Designer & Researcher

From storytelling to systemic design.

Hi, I'm Ali. I am a Senior Product and UI/UX Designer with over six years of experience creating intuitive, scalable solutions for Fintech, Enterprise Banking, and Digital Modernization sectors.

My journey into design is unconventional. I spent my early career as a Journalist and Editor for Agence France-Presse (AFP) and NHK. That experience instilled in me a deep understanding of human psychology, fact-checking, and narrative structure.

Today, I leverage that unique background to excel in user research, qualitative interviewing, and data-driven storytelling. I am passionate about redesigning legacy systems, untangling complex backend logic, and translating strict technical constraints into accessible, WCAG-compliant user journeys within fast-paced Agile environments.
